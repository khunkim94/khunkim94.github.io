About me

Currently pursuing a perfect blend of style and function for a wide range of interactive graphic design. For the past 6 years I've helped global companies, organizations and start-ups reach business goals with user focused, interactive design. I enjoy bringing clarity to complex problems with a people first approach.
I am a complete social animal. Find my work on Behance & Dribbble. Experience my photography skills on Instagram & Flickr.

TECHNICAL SKILLS

As a reluctant graphic designer, I fled from a traditional path and explored my creativity, trough trying my hands on various techniques and software. Software is more than just a tool, and I try to dig deep into it. Today, I knack for critical thinking, empathy and creativity to create viable and impactful solutions using these technologies.
